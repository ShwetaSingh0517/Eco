Sanskar History



Established in 2005, Sanskar Educational Group (SEG) is a Higher Education Institute situated in Ghaziabad, Delhi-NCR. With a vision to groom young minds, we provide them with the best academic environment and opportunities. At SEG, we work hard to develop the smartest and most capable minds and technocrats. We entered the fields of Engineering Technology, Pharmacy, Architecture, and Management in order to support higher education, research, and training initiatives. It boosts and initiates a positive impact on the industrial development and economic expansion of our country and young minds.
